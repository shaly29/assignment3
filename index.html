Some common questions

My flowers are falling off or dying?

Plants might drop flower buds if there is too much water in the soil and roots are suffocated, or if the soil too dry.

What causes leaves to become pale?

The stems and leaves are often pale in color, typically white or yellow. The pale color is caused by a lack of chlorophyll – the pigment in leaves that makes them green.

What causes brown crispy leaves?

Dry air, especially in rooms with central heating, can make their edges a little crispy. Make the air more humid by misting your plants several times a week. Just a light spray will do. Or put them in a kitchen or bathroom where it's naturally steamy.

How do i choose a plant?

Plants are easy way to add color energy and transform your space but which planet is for you. Choosing the right plant.

How do I change the pots?

Choose a new pot that is larger than the current one. Ensure it has drainage holes to allow excess water to escape.

Why are gnats flying around my plant?

Because gnats lay their eggs in the moist soil around houseplants, reducing excess moisture is critical to eliminating them. Avoid overwatering your houseplants, and make sure they have good drainage
